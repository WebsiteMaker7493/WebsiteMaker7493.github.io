Characters

In Outer Wilds you play as a semi aquatic, 4-eyed species known as the Hearthians

There are two other races in Outer Wilds, The Nomai, and an unnamed species referred to as Owlks by the community

Outer Wilds has many characters but 6 that play a greater role

  1. Yourself(Hearthian)
    You are an unamed protagonist referred to as "Hatchling"
  2. Esker(Hearthian)
    An older Hearthian living on Timber Hearths moon. His "instrument" is whistling
  3. Riebeck(Hearthian)
    Timber Hearths archeologist,however a very nervous and anxious astronaut. His instrument is the banjo
  4. Gabbro(Hearthian)
    A laid back astronaut, who is inside the time loop with you. His instrument is the flute
  5. Chert(Hearthian)
    A small, curious, and devoted astronomer of Timber Hearth. His instrument is the drums
  6. Feldspar(Hearthian)
    A brave, daring, and careless astronaut who went missing long ago. His instrument is the harmonica

There are a few more notable characters, but since Outer Wilds is based around
knowledge, its hard to say much without major spoilers

A characters instrument is what helps you find them in the solar system
