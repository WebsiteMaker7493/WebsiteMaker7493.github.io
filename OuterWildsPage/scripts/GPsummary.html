Outer Wilds Gameplay Summary

Outer Wilds is a space exploration game set in a small solar system of 5 main planets

Your goal is to fly around to each planet, their moons, and more, to uncover ancient text
written by a long lost race called the Nomai

Each time loop runs on a 22 minute timer that ends with the solar systems sun going supernova

Every bit of text and lore you uncover is saved on your ships log, which retains data through loops.
The entire game is based around knowledge, no item or upgrade progression

There are 5 Main planets in the game

  1. Timber Hearth
    The homeplanet of your characters species
  2. The Hourglass Twins
    Two mini planets orbiting eachother, connected by a collum of sand, hence the name
  3. Brittle Hollow
    A hollow planet with an black hole in the center and a volcanic moon
  4. Giants Deep
    A gas giant with an ocean inside
  5. Dark Bramble
    A tiny weed-like planet with a hole into its pocket dimensions
