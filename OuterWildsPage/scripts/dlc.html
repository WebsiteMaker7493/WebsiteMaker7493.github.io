Outer Wilds' DLC

Outer wilds has a DLC that near perfectly fits in with the base game

The DLC adds one new planet, or in this case, a spaceship for you to explore

This spaceship, named The Stranger, is home to a new alien species to learn about

The new species has no known official name, so the commmunity has named
them the Owlks, due to their resemblance to Owls and Elks

The DLC expands on the base game not only by story, but also by gameplay,
as it contains horror sequences

The horror sequences dont feel out of place however due to the great atmosphere created
